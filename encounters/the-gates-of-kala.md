# The Gates of Kala — the Silent Sentries of the Underworld

> **A set-piece / threshold encounter.** At **[Kala](../lore/nila-atlas.md)** (a
> city on the north coast of **[Kalikhat](../lore/campaign-overview.md)**) stand
> the **gates of the underworld.** Outside them, standing sentry, is a line of
> **6–10 level-20 champions** — one for each of the **Kalikhat mutations** — who
> are **utterly silent unless the party tries to enter.** From the **6/30
> brainstorm**. Legend: `[settled]` · `[loose]` · `[OPEN]` · 🔒 secret.

---

## 1. The gates `[settled — brainstorm]`

**[Kala](../lore/nila-atlas.md)** (city, Kalikhat north coast, ~X61 Y3) holds the
**gates of the underworld** — a threshold into the deep world beneath Kalikhat,
the archipelago's **Underdark cradle** and land of demons
([overview §5](../lore/campaign-overview.md);
[world-history-timeline](../lore/world-history-timeline.md), the Kalikhat thread,
where the people were driven underground and the surface poisoned).

## 2. The sentries `[settled — brainstorm]`

Standing outside the gates are **6–10 sentinels, each of level 20** — a spread of
high classes: **paladins, fighters, monks, warlocks,** and the like. Each one
**embodies one of the Kalikhat mutations** — the transformations the poisoned
land and the underworld below work on those who dwell there.

- **They are silent.** They do not speak, threaten, or move. A party can walk up,
  study them, and walk away untouched.
- **The trigger is entry.** They act **only if the party tries to pass through the
  gates.** Until then, they are scenery with a pulse — an unmistakable *do not
  enter* written in twenty levels apiece.
- **The read.** Six to ten level-20 guardians is not a fight the party is meant to
  win at the wall; it's a **wall.** The scene's job is dread and deferral — the
  underworld is real, it is *here*, and the door is kept.
