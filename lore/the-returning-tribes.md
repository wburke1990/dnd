# The Returning Tribes — DM Reference (developing thread)

> A proposed thread, not yet hard canon. Ties the [Clay Tablet of Baruch](../handouts/clay-tablet-of-baruch.md)
> and the dragonborn diaspora to a Ten-Lost-Tribes / Book-of-Mormon frame.

---

## The proposal

The **dragonborn who returned to slaughter the elves** are this world's **"Nephites."**

The frame is the **Ten Lost Tribes** ([wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ten_Lost_Tribes))
by way of the **Book of Mormon**: two families flee across the sea before a great
sacking, build a ship, cross the ocean, and settle a far land; one line keeps the
old law, another turns rebellious. Scripture's *"other sheep I have, which are not
of this fold… and there shall be one fold, and one shepherd"* is read as the
scattered tribes who will be gathered.

In Nila that maps onto the **dragonborn diaspora**:

- All dragonborn descend from **one father** (Taniyn), homeland **Lonka** — exactly
  the Baruch tablet's *"all we of the twelve tribes… descended from a single father,"*
  the letter sent **to Lonka** and to **the nine and a half tribes across the great sea.**
- A returning branch of those scattered tribes — the **Nephites** — came back across
  the water and **slaughtered the elves** (fits the anti-colonial spine: the elven
  empire as the wronging power, the ancestral dragonborn returning).
- Today their descendants are the **dragonborn religion on Jotunheim** — a Nephite-mold
  faith of a people who crossed the sea and remember a single father.

*(DM in-joke for David: "remember what you called your cult?" — the connection to
the present-day Jotunheim dragonborn religion.)*
