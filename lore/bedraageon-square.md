## 1. The square `[settled — brainstorm]`

Bedraageon Square is **Maalm's main plaza** — the civic and social center of the
city, where the roads, the market, and the public buildings meet. It is where
**the party was ambushed in session 1**, so the players already know the space;
this is the ground they fought on.

## 2. Bedraageon's grave — the fountain & the plaque `[settled — brainstorm]`

At the heart of the square is **Bedraageon's grave: a fountain with a plaque.**
The plaque carries the founder's epitaph — the "lay of the last survivor"
inscription quoted in full in [maalm-founding.md](maalm-founding.md) (Layer One),
now attributed to **Bedraageon. Dragonborn.**

> ⚠️ **Design tension to resolve (`[OPEN]`).** [maalm-founding.md](maalm-founding.md)
> deliberately makes the grave a **flat marker, flush with the ground, easy to
> walk past** — *no grandeur* — because the reveal is meant to live in **the
> tavern (The Twelve), not the plaza**: "the grave tells them almost nothing; the
> tavern tells them everything." A **fountain** adds exactly the grandeur that
> design was avoiding. Ways to square it: (a) keep the humble flush marker **and**
> add the fountain nearby as ordinary civic dressing; (b) make the fountain itself
> plain and the plaque understated, so it still reads as "easy to miss"; or
> (c) let the fountain replace the flat marker and re-tune the reveal. Left for
> David.

## 3. The ring of civic buildings `[loose — brainstorm]`

The buildings around the square — Maalm's public infrastructure, and a ready set
of scene locations:

- **Meeting house / theater** — a little civic hall that doubles as a playhouse
  (town assembly, announcements, performances).
- **Mead cart** — the square's open-air drink stand. *(Ties into the
  [satyr tavern-keepers](satyr-taverns.md) and the Dragonborn-drinks-free custom.)*
- **Bank house** — money-changing and deposit; a natural [Company](the-company.md)
  touch-point if the party ever follows the coin.
- **Healer house** — the town's infirmary / temple-clinic.
- **Guard tower** — the watch's seat on the square (cf. the **corrupt watch** the
  party meets in Maalm, [the Second Hand ambush](../encounters/the-second-hand-ambush.md)).
- **Blacksmith** — the forge; gear, repairs, and shoeing.
