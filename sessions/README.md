## Running threads

- **The escaped rat man** (S1) — still loose, and the party's real
  shadow: he's **bribing the city-gate guides** to track them. Not yet
  written up in `bestiary/` (to-do).
- **Misplaced suspicion** (S2) — the party thinks the city-gate guides
  answer to **Preem**. They actually answer to the **rat man**. Payoff
  when they untangle it: Preem looks innocent, the rat man looks dangerous.
- **The rat-meat prop** — picked up S1, offered to everything (the
  Set-beast took it; the lepers would be insulted), finally pays off
  feeding the lions in S3.
- **The names ledger** — three of four Amseh tombs cleared; the party is
  carrying their names back to Preem without knowing what names do.
- **Tomb IV** (Amseh IV, Kethara's husband) — still sealed; the Valley's
  capstone, and the natural pickup for Session 4.
