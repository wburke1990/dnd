happened *at the table*, which is sometimes different from the prep in
`encounters/` and `lore/`. Where play diverged from prep (e.g. the
improvised "rat man" and "magic jackal"), the notes flag it.

## Running threads

- **The escaped rat man** (S1) — still loose; who sent it?
- **The city-gate guides** (S2) — party suspects they answer to Preem.
  They're right in spirit.
- **The rat-meat prop** — picked up S1, offered to everything, finally
  pays off feeding the lions in S3.
- **The names ledger** — three of four Amseh tombs cleared; the party is
  carrying their names back to Preem without knowing what names do.
- **Tomb IV** (Amseh IV, Kethara's husband) — still sealed; the Valley's
  capstone, and the natural pickup for Session 4.
