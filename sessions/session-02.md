# Session 02 — The Road to the Valley, and the First Tomb
## Maalm → the Valley of the Kings · Tomb I cleared

---

# RECAP

Commission in hand, the party left Maalm on foot for the **Valley of the
Kings**, out through the oasis gardens and into the rain-shadowed desert
where the sandstone cliffs are honeycombed with the tombs of the Amseh
dynasty.

---

# WHAT HAPPENED

## On the road — the magic jackal

While crossing the desert, the party hunted, and a **hunt-table roll**
turned up a **jackal**. At the table this was elevated from a mundane
scavenger into a **magic jackal** — a talking creature that spoke with the
party.

- The party **fed it rat meat** (the rat-meat prop carries over from
  Session 1 — and unlike everyone else they'll try to feed it, the jackal
  actually *took* it).
- In exchange / in conversation, the jackal **gave them information about
  the lions** waiting in the Valley — a warning, a tip, or a riddle about
  the cave lions that guard one of the tombs. The party went in
  forewarned.

> **Prep note:** The encounter table in `encounters/valley-of-the-kings.md`
> only lists a mundane **Desert Jackal Pack** (d20:3). The talking, helpful
> "magic jackal" was a table improvisation. If it's meant to recur as a
> guide spirit (the Valley is full of jackal-headed Duamutef imagery), it's
> worth promoting to a named entity in the bestiary/lore.

## Suspicion at the city gate

The party came away convinced that the **guides at the city gate are
working with Preem** — that the people who pointed them toward the Valley
(or who handle the supply/charity caravans out to the tombs) are Preem's
eyes, not neutral locals.

- This is the **first crack of distrust** toward Preem. They don't have the
  whole picture yet, but they've correctly sensed that he's managing them.

## The first tomb

The party reached the Valley and **cleared Tomb I** — the first of the four
Amseh tombs. They worked through its puzzle chamber and inner burial
chamber and recovered what the tomb held (per Preem's commission, the
**name** written there).

# DM NOTES & OPEN THREADS

- **Rat meat, ongoing.** Accepted by the jackal. The party now treats it as
  a universal goodwill offering — set up the **lepers** at the Valley
  entrance (who find the offer insulting) and the **cave lions** (Session
  3) as payoff/contrast.
- **The guides = Preem's agents.** The party's suspicion is *correct in
  spirit*. Decide how much to confirm: do the guides report back? Does
  Preem know exactly where the party is at all times? This is good paranoia
  fuel for the colonialism/being-managed theme.
- **The jackal's tip about the lions** paid off in Session 3 — the party
  arrived at the lions' tomb already inclined to parley/feed rather than
  fight.
