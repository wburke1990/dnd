# The First Island (Sam / Blackacre's creation story)

*The peg for Sam's story. Blackacre is Dragonborn — closest to the root of all
the dragon-descended — and by his own mechanic the telling is his: whatever tall
tale Sam gives the party is how it went ([roster](../characters/party-roster.md)).
This is only the nail he hangs it on. Lineage: dragon-people, at the root (see
[lineages & afterlives](../lore/lineages-and-afterlives.md) §10).*

---

> The Dragonborn are the oldest. The first dragon fell from the star and became
> the first island, and the Dragonborn were her first children — before the
> dwarves, before the elves, before any of the rest. They were there when there
> was one island and one people, and they remember the water better than anyone.

---

*The rest is Sam's to tell. His tall tales become canon; this only fixes where
he starts.*
