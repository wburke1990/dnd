# The First Giant (Sarric's creation story)

*The story Sarric's people — the Firbolgs, the small kin of the giants — tell
about how the world was made. Real-world root: the Norse **Ymir**, the world
built from the body of the first giant. This is the version Sarric was raised
on. Lineage: the nameless, earth (see
[lineages & afterlives](../lore/lineages-and-afterlives.md) §5).*

---

> In the first days there was one giant. She was the size of the whole world
> that had not been made yet.
>
> She lay down and did not get up. Her body became the land. The mountains are
> her. The high stone is her.
>
> Grass came up through her. Trees came to the grass. The bees came to the
> trees.
>
> This is where we go when we are done. The great ones lie down and become new
> hills. The rest of us become the small green life on them — the meadow, the
> trees at the snowline, the bees.
>
> A giant has no name to leave behind. So before she lies down the last time
> she sets her hand flat to the rock and holds it there. Walk the high places
> and you will find the hands in the stone. Every hand was someone.
