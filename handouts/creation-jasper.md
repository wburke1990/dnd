# One People (Jasper's creation story)

*The story Jasper was raised on — a common-descent origin turned to a colonial
wound, drawn from his own character concept ([roster](../characters/party-roster.md)).
Lineage: dragon-people, judged in the sky — which he does not yet know sets him
apart from Sarric and Pax (see
[lineages & afterlives](../lore/lineages-and-afterlives.md) §7).*

---

> In the beginning there was one dragon. She fell from the star and became the
> first island, and her children were the first people.
>
> They were one people. They spread out over the water, and the world changed
> them — the world here changes fast — and the children of one island grew
> unlike the children of the next.
>
> They forgot they had ever been one. Now they take each other's land, and each
> other's names, and call each other lesser. They are cousins and they do not
> know it.
>
> When they die they go up to be weighed by the one who made their line. The one
> who made every line was the same dragon. No one remembers this either.
