# Shop & Price Reference

A quick at-the-table price list for when the party walks into a store,
market, tavern, stable, or temple. Values are **standard D&D 5e**
(PHB/DMG) so they slot cleanly into the Maalm/Nila campaign's gp/sp/cp
economy. Treat them as a baseline — bump prices in remote/colonial outposts,
drop them where a good is plentiful, and let a good Persuasion check or a
slow market shave 10–20%.

**Coin:** 1 pp = 10 gp · 1 gp = 10 sp = 100 cp · 1 sp = 10 cp.
(ep = electrum = 5 sp, rarely used.)

## Services — Hirelings & Spellcasting

**Spellcasting at a temple/mage** (typical, scales with spell level):
- Minor heal / 1st-level spell — ~10–30 gp
- *Lesser Restoration*, *Remove Curse*, 3rd-level — ~150–250 gp
- *Raise Dead* — 1,250 gp (incl. 500 gp diamond)
- *Greater Restoration* — ~450 gp · *Resurrection* — material ~1,000 gp+
- *Identify* — ~20 gp + cost. Rarer/higher-level casting is by favor, not coin.

(Caster also charges for any consumed material component — pass that cost to
the buyer.)

## Magic Items (rough buy/sell guidance, DMG)

Magic items are rarely on open shelves — treat these as ceilings for a
black-market fixer or a generous patron, and never as a guaranteed buy.

| Rarity | Typical value | Found at character level |
|---|---|---|
| Common | 50–100 gp | 1+ |
| Uncommon | 101–500 gp | 1+ |
| Rare | 501–5,000 gp | 5+ |
| Very rare | 5,001–50,000 gp | 11+ |
| Legendary | 50,001+ gp | 17+ |

Consumables (potions, scrolls) sell/buy at roughly **half** the table value.
A 1st-level spell scroll ~25–75 gp; *Potion of Healing* tiers: common 50 gp,
greater 150 gp, superior 450 gp, supreme 1,350 gp.
