# The Copaa Lament — the River Run Foul (Player Handout)

*A lament for [Copaa](../lore/nila-atlas.md) — the downstream town whose river and fields are poisoned by the [Company's mine runoff at Haals](../encounters/the-haals-runoff.md), its people driven out by the blight. Copaa's region is a **fallen gnome kingdom** (its own mythology, in progress). Use as a scroll the party finds, or a lament Copaa's elders recite as they point upstream. The lines carry the eco-atrocity's mythic register — the river run to blood, the fields dead.*

*Real-world source: "The Admonitions of Ipuwer" (Papyrus Leiden I 344), Middle Egyptian lament of a land in chaos; selected lines. Verbatim to the chosen rendering; translations of the Admonitions vary.*

---

```
Lo, the river is blood, yet men drink of it;
men shrink from people, and thirst after water.
Lo, the desert spreads across the land, the nomes are laid waste,
and grain has perished on every side.
Lo, that which was seen yesterday has perished;
the land is left to its weariness, like the cutting of flax.
```
