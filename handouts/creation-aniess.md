# The Sunne That Falls and Rises (Aniess's creation story)

*The story Aniess recites — the faith of **Sunne**, the religion of Kuru and
its Order, the one she was raised in. It is borrowed: her heart is full of
someone else's convictions, and this is not her people's story. Her own — the
**Serpent of Punt** — is hidden even from her. See
[lineages & afterlives](../lore/lineages-and-afterlives.md) §10.*

---

> The Sunne fell burning from the star. Where it struck the sea the first land
> cooled out of the fire.
>
> It made the world in that burning, and then it died, because it always dies.
> It is born again at every turning of the star, and each time the world is
> remade in its new light.
>
> We are its work. We are made to burn for it and to be remade by it. To give
> yourself to the fire is the whole of the faith.
