# Available Demons — Stat-Block Roster

The demons we have access to as stat blocks, for casting NPCs and encounters.
This is the **palette**: when a scene needs a demon, pick from here so it's a
block we can actually run. Sorted by Challenge Rating, highest first.

> Already in canon: **Mul** (Preem's right hand) and **Manizheh** (the
> dream-quest demon-wife) are both **Yochlols**, reflavored off Lolth — the
> setting's standard "demon wearing a person." Preem's bound army also draws on
> **Quasit, Barlgura, Shadow Demon, Vrock, Nalfeshnee, Chasme** (see
> [`characters/preems-demons.md`](../characters/preems-demons.md)).
