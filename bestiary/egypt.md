# Egypt — Wandering Encounter Bestiary

Sun-blasted river kingdom for the upcoming Egypt-themed quest. Wandering
encounters here are rolled on the **d20 Egypt encounter table** below.

*Tags:* egypt-quest; wandering encounter region

Rolls **1–9** are mundane beasts (safe to hunt/eat). Rolls **10–20** are
mythological — most carry a **BOON** for the right interaction and a **CURSE**
for killing or eating them carelessly.

## Mundane Beasts (1–9)

### 1 · Nile Crocodile
*Beast (Large)* — **Beast, CR 2** · Unaligned
**AC** 14 · **HP** 45 · **STR** 21 **DEX** 9 **CON** 17 **INT** 2 **WIS** 10 **CHA** 7

Spd 30 ft, swim 50 ft. Bite +6 (3d10+5 piercing; grapple, DC 16 STR escape). Hold Breath 30 min. Stealth +5 in water (advantage). Drags grappled prey underwater. Yields ~4 days rations + hide.

*Tags:* egypt-mundane; d20:1; river ambusher

### 2 · Hippopotamus
*Beast (Large)* — **Beast, CR 4** · Unaligned
**AC** 12 · **HP** 76 · **STR** 23 **DEX** 8 **CON** 18 **INT** 2 **WIS** 11 **CHA** 7

Spd 40 ft, swim 40 ft. Bite +9 (3d8+6 piercing). Trample (Multiattack: if it moves 20+ ft straight toward target, target DC 16 STR or prone; bonus stomp 2d10+6). Most dangerous mundane beast in Egypt — kills more travelers than crocodiles. Yields ~10 days rations.

*Tags:* egypt-mundane; d20:2; territorial; pairs near calves

### 3 · Desert Jackal Pack
*Beast (Small, pack of 2d4)* — **Beast, CR 0 each** · Unaligned
**AC** 12 · **HP** 8 · **STR** 8 **DEX** 15 **CON** 11 **INT** 3 **WIS** 12 **CHA** 6

Spd 40 ft. Bite +1 (1d4-1 piercing). Pack Tactics (adv when ally adjacent). Keen Hearing & Smell (adv on Perception relying on hearing/smell). Will scavenge unguarded camps overnight — steals 1d4 days rations on a successful Stealth check vs. watchkeeper's Passive Perception.

*Tags:* egypt-mundane; d20:3; nocturnal scavenger

### 4 · Horned Viper
*Beast (Tiny)* — **Beast, CR 1/8** · Unaligned
**AC** 13 · **HP** 5 · **STR** 2 **DEX** 16 **CON** 11 **INT** 1 **WIS** 10 **CHA** 3

Spd 30 ft, burrow 5 ft. Bite +5 (1 piercing + DC 11 CON or 2d4 poison, half on save). Sand Camouflage: advantage on Stealth (+8) when buried. Blindsight 10 ft. Typically ambushes barefoot/sandaled targets — a foot in the wrong dune.

*Tags:* egypt-mundane; d20:4; ambush; sand-buried

### 5 · Scarab Beetle Swarm
*Swarm of Tiny Beasts (Medium)* — **Beast, CR 1/2** · Unaligned
**AC** 12 · **HP** 22 · **STR** 3 **DEX** 13 **CON** 10 **INT** 1 **WIS** 7 **CHA** 1

Spd 20 ft, climb 20 ft, burrow 5 ft. Bites +3 (4d4 piercing, or 2d4 at half HP or fewer). Resistance: bludgeoning, piercing, slashing. Immune: charmed, frightened, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, prone, restrained, stunned. Devours unguarded rations in 1d4 rounds — emerges from sand or sarcophagus seams.

*Tags:* egypt-mundane; d20:5; emerges from ruins or rations

### 6 · Hamadryas Baboon Troop
*Beast (Small, troop of 1d4+3)* — **Beast, CR 0 each** · Unaligned
**AC** 12 · **HP** 3 · **STR** 8 **DEX** 14 **CON** 11 **INT** 4 **WIS** 12 **CHA** 6

Spd 30 ft, climb 30 ft. Fist +1 (1d4-1 bludgeoning). Thrown Rock +3 (1d4+1 bludgeoning, range 20/60). Pack Tactics. Won't usually press a fight — opportunistic. Stealth +4 to snatch a shiny item or a day of rations and bolt up the nearest cliff.

*Tags:* egypt-mundane; d20:6; thieves; cliff escape

### 7 · Caracal
*Beast (Small)* — **Beast, CR 1/4** · Unaligned
**AC** 13 · **HP** 13 · **STR** 10 **DEX** 16 **CON** 11 **INT** 3 **WIS** 14 **CHA** 7

Spd 40 ft, climb 20 ft. Bite +5 (1d6+3 piercing). Pounce: 20 ft running jump, target DC 13 STR or prone, bonus claw +5 (1d4+3 slashing). Stealth +6. Solitary ambusher from rocks or palm crowns. Yields ~1 day rations + pelt (tufted ears prized).

*Tags:* egypt-mundane; d20:7; solitary ambusher

### 8 · Egyptian Vulture Flock
*Beast (Medium, flock of 2d4)* — **Beast, CR 0 each** · Unaligned
**AC** 10 · **HP** 5 · **STR** 7 **DEX** 10 **CON** 13 **INT** 2 **WIS** 12 **CHA** 4

Spd 10 ft, fly 60 ft. Beak +1 (1d4-2 piercing). Keen Sight (advantage on Perception relying on sight). Will not engage a healthy group, but mobs the wounded and snatches exposed food at rest. A circling flock is an omen — something below has died, or is about to.

*Tags:* egypt-mundane; d20:8; circling = portent; daylight

### 9 · Saharan Oryx
*Beast (Medium)* — **Beast, CR 1/2** · Unaligned
**AC** 11 · **HP** 15 · **STR** 16 **DEX** 11 **CON** 13 **INT** 2 **WIS** 10 **CHA** 6

Spd 50 ft. Gore +5 (1d6+3 piercing). Charge: if it moves 20+ ft straight toward target before goring, extra 1d6 piercing and DC 13 STR or knocked prone. Long horns can skewer — desert wisdom says a wounded oryx kills more hunters than an unwounded lion. Yields ~5 days rations + horns (50 gp pair).

*Tags:* egypt-mundane; d20:9; herd of 1d6+2; valuable kill

## Mythological Beasts (10–20)

### 10 · Medjed
*Monstrosity (Small, fish)* — **Monstrosity, CR 5** · Lawful Neutral
**AC** 16 · **HP** 60 · **STR** 10 **DEX** 18 **CON** 14 **INT** 14 **WIS** 18 **CHA** 12

Spd 0 ft, swim 60 ft. Smite +8 ranged 60 ft (4d8 force, no save, ignores cover). Invisible while submerged. Resistance to nonmagical damage. The 'Smiter' of the Book of the Dead — domed head, stubby legs, never seen clearly. BOON: eating its flesh = 1 casting of Eyebite per long rest. CURSE: killing without ritual = blinded 1d4 days.

*Tags:* egypt-mythological; d20:10; Nile depths; ritual required

### 11 · Abtu and Anet
*Celestial (Medium, paired fish)* — **Celestial, CR 3 each** · Lawful Good
**AC** 14 · **HP** 38 · **STR** 14 **DEX** 16 **CON** 14 **INT** 8 **WIS** 16 **CHA** 14

Spd 0 ft, swim 60 ft. Headbutt +5 (1d8+2 bludgeoning + 1d6 radiant). Solar Herald: enemies of Ra (undead, fiends) within 30 ft have disadvantage on attack rolls. Always appear as a pair, swimming ahead of any solar omen. Will flee rather than fight. BOON: eating one = adv on initiative permanently. CURSE: eating both = compelled east at every dawn until sunrise (CON save to resist).

*Tags:* egypt-mythological; d20:11; pair; flee combat

### 12 · Bennu
*Celestial (Large, heron)* — **Celestial, CR 7** · Neutral Good
**AC** 15 · **HP** 90 · **STR** 12 **DEX** 18 **CON** 16 **INT** 16 **WIS** 18 **CHA** 20

Spd 20 ft, fly 80 ft. Beak +8 (2d6+4 piercing + 2d6 fire). Rebirth: at 0 HP, bursts in 3d6 fire 20 ft radius (DC 15 DEX half), reforms from ashes in 1d4 days. Immune fire; resist radiant. Original of the phoenix myth. BOON: drinking its tears (no kill) restores 1 expended Hit Die / long rest. CURSE: killing it = party gains NO long rest benefits for 7 days.

*Tags:* egypt-mythological; d20:12; appears at dawn; tears > kill

### 13 · Apis Bull
*Celestial (Large, bull)* — **Celestial, CR 5** · Lawful Good
**AC** 16 · **HP** 102 · **STR** 22 **DEX** 10 **CON** 18 **INT** 10 **WIS** 16 **CHA** 18

Spd 40 ft. Gore +9 (3d8+6 piercing). Trample (charge 20+ ft, DC 16 STR or prone + 2d10+6 bludgeoning). Divine Aura: any creature that damages it must DC 16 WIS save or be frightened of it 1 min. Good-aligned allies within 30 ft gain +1 AC. Marked by specific white patches — easily mistaken for ordinary cattle by the uninitiated. BOON: eating its meat = +1 CON permanently. CURSE: every Egyptian-aligned NPC reads the eater as desecrated on sight; temples refuse sanctuary.

*Tags:* egypt-mythological; d20:13; mistakable as cattle; CHA-rep curse

### 14 · Petsuchos
*Monstrosity (Huge, crocodile)* — **Monstrosity, CR 8** · Neutral
**AC** 17 · **HP** 126 · **STR** 22 **DEX** 8 **CON** 19 **INT** 6 **WIS** 12 **CHA** 14

Spd 30 ft, swim 50 ft. Multiattack: Bite + Tail. Bite +9 (3d10+6 piercing + grapple DC 17). Tail +9 (2d8+6 bludgeoning + DC 17 STR or prone, 10 ft reach). Sobek's Favor: any acid or poison damage instead heals it. Adorned in gold and gems (~3000 gp if stripped). BOON: eating its heart raw = swim speed + water breathing for 30 days. CURSE: killing without offering = every body of water deeper than waist deals 1d6 necrotic on entry, for a year.

*Tags:* egypt-mythological; d20:14; sacred to Sobek; treasure-bearing

### 15 · Serpopard Pair
*Beast (Large, chaos-touched, pair)* — **Beast, CR 4 each** · Neutral
**AC** 14 · **HP** 68 · **STR** 17 **DEX** 16 **CON** 14 **INT** 4 **WIS** 14 **CHA** 8

Spd 50 ft. Long-Neck Bite +6 (2d8+3 piercing, reach 10 ft). Pounce (20 ft, DC 13 STR or prone + bonus claw +6 / 1d6+3). Stealth +6. Hunt in pairs from opposite sides. The chaos at the edge of order — predynastic boundary beast. NO curse for hunting; their meat is safe and grants +2 Perception checks for 1d4 days. Boundary of civilization made flesh.

*Tags:* egypt-mythological; d20:15; pair flank; no curse — fair game

### 16 · Sha (Set-Animal)
*Fiend (Medium)* — **Fiend, CR 6** · Chaotic Evil
**AC** 15 · **HP** 75 · **STR** 16 **DEX** 18 **CON** 16 **INT** 10 **WIS** 12 **CHA** 14

Spd 50 ft. Forked Bite +7 (2d6+3 piercing + 1d6 lightning + DC 14 CON or stunned until end of its next turn). Sandstorm Step: bonus action teleport 30 ft in a dust cloud, 1/short rest. Resist lightning; immune to environmental sandstorm effects. Unidentifiable forked-tail beast of Set. BOON: eating its liver = adv on saves vs charmed/frightened for 1 week. CURSE: sandstorms follow eater 3d6 days, disadv on ranged attacks outdoors.

*Tags:* egypt-mythological; d20:16; Set-aligned; desert ambush

### 17 · Bastet's Cat
*Celestial (Tiny, cat)* — **Celestial, CR 1 (deceptively dangerous)** · Chaotic Good
**AC** 15 · **HP** 18 · **STR** 8 **DEX** 18 **CON** 12 **INT** 14 **WIS** 18 **CHA** 16

Spd 40 ft, climb 30 ft. Bite +6 (1d4 piercing + 1d6 radiant). Cat's Curse (reaction when attacked): attacker DC 14 CHA save or has disadvantage on all rolls for 1 hour. Telepathy 60 ft with cat-kin. Found in any temple district; indistinguishable from a normal cat to non-divine senses. NO BOON. CURSE for killing one: disadv on ALL DEX saves until atonement — return 7 kittens to a temple of Bastet.

*Tags:* egypt-mythological; d20:17; looks like a normal cat; do not eat

### 18 · Ammit's Spawn
*Fiend (Large, lion-hippo-crocodile)* — **Fiend, CR 9** · Lawful Evil
**AC** 17 · **HP** 142 · **STR** 22 **DEX** 12 **CON** 20 **INT** 8 **WIS** 14 **CHA** 10

Spd 30 ft, swim 40 ft. Multiattack: Bite + Claw. Bite +10 (3d10+6 piercing + 2d6 necrotic + DC 17 CHA or max HP reduced by necrotic damage dealt until long rest). Claw +10 (2d8+6 slashing). Devour Soul: a creature reduced to 0 HP by this beast cannot be resurrected by anything short of Wish or True Resurrection. BOON: eating its heart = immune to frightened for 30 days. LONG-FUSE CURSE: eater's heart grows heavy as the feather of Ma'at — at death, no resurrection magic works on them.

*Tags:* egypt-mythological; d20:18; soul-eater; permanent stakes

### 19 · Uraeus Cobra
*Celestial (Medium, snake)* — **Celestial, CR 4** · Lawful Neutral
**AC** 15 · **HP** 52 · **STR** 12 **DEX** 18 **CON** 14 **INT** 10 **WIS** 16 **CHA** 14

Spd 30 ft, climb 20 ft. Bite +7 (1d6+4 piercing + 4d6 fire + DC 14 CON or paralyzed 1 min, save end of turn). Flame Hood: any creature within 10 ft at start of its turn takes 1d6 fire. Resist fire. The rearing cobra of pharaonic crowns. BOON: milking (not killing) = one-time Fire Breath (3d6, 15 ft cone). CURSE for killing: every snake within 5 miles becomes hostile to the party for the rest of the quest.

*Tags:* egypt-mythological; d20:19; milk don't kill; coiled near regalia

### 20 · Akhekh
*Monstrosity (Large, winged serpent-griffin)* — **Monstrosity, CR 7** · Chaotic Neutral
**AC** 16 · **HP** 105 · **STR** 19 **DEX** 16 **CON** 17 **INT** 7 **WIS** 12 **CHA** 10

Spd 30 ft, fly 60 ft. Multiattack: Beak + Tail Sting. Beak +8 (2d8+4 piercing). Tail Sting +8 (1d8+4 piercing + 3d6 poison, DC 15 CON half). Sandstorm Aura: 15 ft cone in direction faced; ranged attacks into/out of cone at disadvantage. Resist poison. Chaos-beast of the deep desert — fair game. NO CURSE. BOON: feathers woven into a cloak grant fire resistance 1d4 days per feather. WARNING: its lair is invariably trapped by Set's priests, who use it as an early-warning system.

*Tags:* egypt-mythological; d20:20; lair often trapped; cloak materials
