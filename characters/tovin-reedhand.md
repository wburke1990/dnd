# Brother Tovin Reedhand — friend NPC (the Verdant Fist)

[Pax](pax.md)'s former monastery friend and the straight man to his prophet:
gentle, practical, and afraid of most animals larger than a goat. A lovable
coward and monk bureaucrat who **misses Pax deeply.**

> **In one line:** he'd be thrilled and terrified to see Pax — a good man who
> should absolutely not be allowed near municipal infrastructure.

**Role:** Pax's former monastery friend
**Type:** Lovable coward / monk bureaucrat / straight man
**Relationship to Pax:** Childhood friend who misses him deeply

## Who he is

Tovin was Pax's closest friend at the Verdant Fist. Where Pax was intense and
visionary, Tovin was gentle, practical, and afraid of most animals larger than a
goat. He helped Pax sneak out food, covered for his early "incidents," and once
lied to the abbot about why three pigs had been painted with sacred sigils.

## How to play him

The comedy of loyalty against better judgment. He loves Pax and is plainly,
constantly alarmed by him — the friend who'd take the blame again in a heartbeat
and beg Pax, in the same breath, to please not do the thing he's obviously about
to do. Play him as the table's reality check: the one who remembers the pigs.

- **"Sweet Reedhand."**
- **"My least treasonous brother."**

A good man who should absolutely not be allowed near municipal infrastructure.
