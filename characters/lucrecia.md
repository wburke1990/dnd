# Lucrecia — an old-family noble of Maalm

> **Elite-caste NPC**, improvised at the table in
> [Session 6](../sessions/session-06.md) and drawn out in
> [Session 7](../sessions/session-07.md). A wealthy woman of Maalm's founders'
> quarter who became the party's way into [the mill](../encounters/the-mill.md)
> — she has her garden grain milled there, and lent her servant Daniel to guide
> them. A patron of the [al Qahtani](the-children.md) healers, and a gossip.
> `[OPEN]` any tie to the [Second Foundation](../lore/the-second-foundation.md).
>
> Legend: `[settled]` · `[loose]` · `[OPEN]` · 🔒 DM secret.

---

## Who she is

- `[settled]` Of Maalm's **elite caste** — the old families of the gated
  founders' quarter (the walled star fortress; see
  [the elven stronghold](../lore/maalm-the-elven-stronghold.md)). `[loose]`
  Read her as an **old-family elf** unless you'd rather place her otherwise.
- `[settled]` **Wealthy, with a mansion and servants.** The party rested in
  her **servants' quarters** while she finished errands in the city (S7). Her
  man **Daniel** runs her household errands, the grain among them.
- `[settled]` She grows **grain in her garden** and has it milled at the
  founder houses' [garden-grain grinder](../encounters/the-mill.md) for her
  bread — which is how she knows the mill.
- `[settled]` Keeps up **skin treatments** with the [al Qahtani](the-children.md),
  Maalm's finest healers, and complains about how expensive her skin is to
  maintain.
- `[OPEN]` Her house name, where her mansion sits, and any tie to the
  [Second Foundation](../lore/the-second-foundation.md), the founders'-quarter
  elite's inner faction.

## Manner

- `[settled]` **Classist.** Given the chance she maps Maalm by rank: the
  walled star fortress inside, the nicer neighborhoods around the walls, and
  the **"bad neighborhoods"** to the south, which she talks about with
  contempt. She was disdainful that the al Qahtani spend their work on
  **charity in the lower city**, treating the low caste. The party read her as
  classist and had it right.
- `[settled]` **Transactional, and wary of being played.** She **approved of
  the two fingers** Jasper took off Ratatat: the maiming told her the fight
  was real, not a staged show run to con a rich woman out of her gratitude and
  coin.
- `[settled]` **Petty.** She asked the party to go **rough up a neighbor**
  until they changed their flowers — the **flowers errand**, an open hook.
- `[settled]` **A gossip.** What she hears, she spreads. Daniel is carrying
  the whole mill story back to her, so the party's fight in the forbidden
  tunnels will likely become **talk among the elite**.

## What she's given the party

- **The way to the mill** — her servant **Daniel** guided them in on his
  afternoon grain run, clean cover behind a delivery
  ([the mill](../encounters/the-mill.md)).
- **A short rest** in her mansion's servants' quarters (S7).
- **50 gp** (S6) meant for a **healing potion** — offered first to Aniess, who
  ignored her, then to **Sarric**, who **pocketed it** and bought nothing. He
  owes a potion that doesn't exist.
- **The flowers errand** — an open job if the party wants her favor, or her
  coin.

## Daniel — her servant

- `[settled]` Makes Lucrecia's **grain runs to the mill** and guided the party
  there. He put them up in the servants' quarters for their rest and is eager
  to relay everything to her.
