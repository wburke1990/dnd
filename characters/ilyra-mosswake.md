# Master Ilyra Mosswake — mentor NPC (the Verdant Fist)

The monk who **trained [Pax](pax.md)** at the **Verdant Fist** — and then **voted
to exile him.** A stern mentor / disappointed-parent figure: older, formidable,
and the one person whose judgment still lands on Pax like weather.

> **In one line:** she did not hate him when he left — and that may be what makes
> it worse. Pax would be happy to see her. Then immediately defensive.

**Role:** Former teacher at the Verdant Fist monastery
**Type:** Stern mentor / disappointed parent figure
**Relationship to Pax:** She trained him. She also voted to exile him.

## Who she is

Master Ilyra is an older monk who taught Pax breathwork, restraint, balance, and
survival in the deep wilds. She loved Pax as a student and believed he
was gifted — but she also believed his **Green Revelation** made him dangerous.
She did not hate him when he left. That may be what makes it worse.

## How to play her

Not a villain and not a forgiving elder — the harder thing, the teacher who was
right *and* loved him. She holds both at once without flinching, which is exactly
what Pax can't do in her presence. Around her his prophet's certainty curdles
into a student's defensiveness; she's the wound he reinterpreted rather than
healed (cf. the Verdant Fist note in [pax.md](pax.md)), given a face and a voice.

## What Pax calls her

- **"Root-Mother Ilyra"** — when sincere.
- **"Agent of Stone-Thinking"** — when upset.
