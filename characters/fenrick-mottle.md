# Old Fenrick Mottle — mentor NPC (post-exile alchemy)

The swamp hermit who found [Pax](pax.md) half-starved and ranting at a pond after
the exile — and taught him alchemy. A weird-uncle / swamp-alchemist / comic
menace, and a frankly **bad influence** Pax adores.

> **In one line:** probably wanted in three counties for selling "truth mud,"
> and the closest thing to family Pax found after the Verdant Fist cast him out.

**Role:** Swamp hermit / illegal herbalist / bad influence
**Type:** Weird uncle / swamp alchemist / comic menace
**Relationship to Pax:** Taught him alchemy after exile

## Who he is

After Pax was exiled, Fenrick found him half-starved, ranting at a pond. Fenrick
is an old swamp-dwelling herbalist who makes salves, poisons, poultices, tonics,
fungus wine, and things that have no business bubbling. He taught Pax how to use
**Alchemist's Supplies** and how to tell the difference between medicine, poison,
and *"depends on dosage."*

Fenrick is probably wanted in three counties for selling **"truth mud."**

## How to play him

The fun and the danger are the same thing. He's warm, delighted, and entirely
without caution — the uncle who hands a kid something bubbling and says *try it.*
He gave Pax real skill and a worse sense of consequences; lean into both. Half
his menace is that he's never once meant any harm.

## What Pax calls him

- **"Uncle Fen."**

## How he sees Pax

*"Nice lad. Glows too much. Talks to moss. Good hands."*
